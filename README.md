# Harbor of Echoes

An investigative narrative webapp where players reconstruct a fictional disappearance using evidence, timelines, maps, and a visual case board.

## Premise

In a remote coastal town, an environmental investigator disappears days before exposing irregularities tied to a tidal energy company. Players receive partial access to the case archive and must determine whether they are dealing with an accident, a cover-up, a staged disappearance, or murder.

## Core Experience

- Review evidence across multiple formats
- Build links between people, places, events, and documents
- Track contradictions and uncertain timelines
- Form and revise hypotheses as new information unlocks
- Submit a final theory and receive a narrative outcome

## Product Pillars

- Atmospheric investigation rather than generic CRUD
- Rich information design with a cinematic tone
- Layered progression driven by discovery and inference
- Systems that support ambiguity instead of obvious solutions

## Planned App Modules

- `Case Dashboard`: current objectives, recent findings, progress
- `Evidence Vault`: searchable archive for documents, photos, messages, recordings
- `Case Board`: visual workspace for connections and annotations
- `Timeline`: event reconstruction with uncertainty and contradictions
- `Entity Profiles`: people, organizations, and locations
- `Map View`: spatial context for movement and sightings
- `Hypothesis Lab`: arguments, evidence for and against, confidence tracking
- `Final Report`: theory submission and ending logic

## Initial Scope

- One fully playable case
- 15 to 25 pieces of evidence
- 6 to 8 key entities
- 10 to 15 timeline events
- Manual evidence linking on the case board
- Final theory submission
