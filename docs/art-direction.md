# Harbor of Echoes Art Direction

## Visual Goal

The project should feel like an investigative archive built from wet paper, cold steel, and municipal secrecy rather than a clean SaaS dashboard. The visual identity needs tension, atmosphere, and editorial restraint.

## Core Mood

- storm-beaten Atlantic coastline
- sodium harbor lights in fog
- government paperwork under low light
- industrial infrastructure under stress
- civic decay hidden behind order

## Texture Language

- damp paper grain
- scratched acrylic overlays
- low-contrast scan artifacts
- faded stamps and institutional marks
- soft lens haze in environmental photography

## Typography Direction

- display: literary, severe, editorial serif
- interface: practical sans with density and clarity
- monospace: archival or terminal tone for IDs, logs, and timestamps

## Composition Rules

- preserve negative space for UI overlays
- avoid crowded scenes with too many actors
- use asymmetry and off-center framing
- favor atmosphere over spectacle
- let weather and surfaces do visual storytelling

## Things To Avoid

- neon cyberpunk tropes
- glossy startup gradients
- bright cartoon crime vibes
- over-detailed collage chaos
- generic detective iconography like magnifying glasses and fedoras
